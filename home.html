Why Bohol

Beyond the Tourist Trail

Bohol is famed for the Chocolate Hills and tarsiers — but the province holds far more. Tucked between forested ridges and coral-fringed coves are villages, waterfalls, and sanctuaries that most travelers never reach. We've mapped them so you don't have to.

Each destination has been personally visited, documented, and curated to ensure it remains unspoiled — and unforgettable.
